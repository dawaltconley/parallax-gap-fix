Parallax Gap Fix

This parallax page works fine without scrollbars, or with the default Mac scrollbars for trackpads, which do not change the width of the scrolling element.

Try forcing scrollbars

On Mac, you can do this by going to Settings > General and setting "Show scroll bars" to "Always." This also happens under the default settings whenever a USB mouse is connected.

Do you see it now?

There should be a strip of white space to the left of this parallax element. This is because the scrollbar changes the inner width of an element, but not the total size, in which the 3D space is rendered.

How do I fix it?

You can try some of my fixes using the menu in the top left. Unfortunately, none of them are perfect. Safari seems to render 3D space differently than every other major browser. Fixes 1 and 3 work everywhere except Safari. Fixes 2 and 4 only work on Safari.

At the moment, fix 3 seems to be the best compromise all around
